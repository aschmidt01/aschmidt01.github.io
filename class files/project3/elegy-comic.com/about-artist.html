Illustrator, mac & cheese enthusiast, friend.

Andrea is currently Los Angeles based, and completing her BFA at Otis College of Art and Design. She's really into animation, and this has had a profound influence on her work in terms of style and theatricality. She hopes that this is evident within Elegy.

With a backlog of old narratives and new ideas constantly rearing their little heads in her mind, Andrea plans on giving more of them the Elegy treatment post-grad.
